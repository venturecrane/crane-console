# Strategic Planning

## Capital Allocation Principles

1. **Self-funding preferred** - Ventures should fund themselves via operations
2. **Shared infrastructure amortized** - Shared workers and databases benefit all ventures
3. **Kill fast** - Don't prop up failing ventures with good-venture profits
4. **Reinvest in winners** - Profitable ventures get growth capital

## Portfolio Evaluation Framework

| Dimension         | Question                                    |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Revenue potential | What's the realistic upside?                |
| Time to revenue   | How long until positive cash flow?          |
| Capital required  | How much investment to reach profitability? |
| Risk profile      | What could kill this?                       |
| Strategic fit     | Does it make other ventures stronger?       |

## Risk Register

| Risk                      | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation                  |
| ------------------------- | ---------- | ------ | --------------------------- |

| Liability exposure        | Low        | High   | Insurance, proper contracts |
