# Silicon Crane

## What It Is

Validation-as-a-Service (VaaS) - structured 30-day sprints that help founders and investors determine whether a business idea deserves to exist.

## The Problem We Solve

Founders waste months building products nobody wants. Investors fund ideas with no market evidence. The gap between "idea" and "company" is filled with expensive guesswork.

## Target Market

**Primary:** Founders with ideas needing validation before significant investment
**Secondary:** Investors wanting validation data before committing capital
**Tertiary:** Accelerators/incubators seeking pre-validation infrastructure

## Value Proposition

Replace months of guesswork with a structured 30-day sprint that produces:

- Clear **Go / Kill / Pivot** decision
- Real market evidence (not surveys or opinions)
- Permanent Learning Ledger documenting what was tested
- Actionable next steps regardless of outcome

## Service Model

### Sprint Structure

| Phase   | Duration  | Output                                   |
| ------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Intake  | Day 1-3   | Hypothesis defined, success criteria set |
| Design  | Day 4-7   | Experiment designed, assets prepared     |
| Run     | Day 8-25  | Live market test, data collection        |
| Analyze | Day 26-28 | Results synthesis                        |
| Deliver | Day 29-30 | Decision memo, Learning Ledger entry     |

### Deliverables

- Structured experiment design
- Live market test execution
- Decision memo with Go/Kill/Pivot recommendation
- Learning Ledger entry (permanent record)
- Debrief call
