# Captain's Reflection

Date: 2026-05-10
Window: 2026-01-13 to 2026-05-09

---

## 1. What do I believe now that I didn't believe on 2026-01-13?

If I recall, we were thinking we could focus solely on developing products that we would use to generate revenue first and then offer as SaaS. However, the runway required to do that was longer than expected, so we have to pivot to the smd.services option, which I would not have thought we could do, but the development of the enterprise and harnessing of the AI agents has made that possible.

## 2. What am I tired of and want to delete?

Delete? Nothing yet. We learn and make progress and get better. We need to get smd.services to a point where it's generating sustaining revenue. Once there, all current ventures remain interesting to pursue.

## 3. What have I been avoiding and why?

Generating content and marketing campaigns on social media. We will need it, but it has the most unknown variables. I know it can be done, we just need to have the opportunity to focus on it. There have been other priorities we have been working, like standing up smd.services.

## 4. What am I proud of and want to compound?

I am proud of the whole enterprise development to date. So much so that we chose to publish our experience on venturecrane.com for the community to see.

## 5. What is the next bet?

Right now, we are putting everything on smd.services. That is our make or break at this point. If that does not succeed and generate sustaining revenue, then I don't know what will happen.
